My Story

    Born in Boston, MA and growing up i was obsessed with tinkering, specifically with cars, so much so i ended up being a mechanic for 9 years. I loved modifying them and figuring out what the fault codes were when they would show up, eventually i wanted something more. I discovered software development and it immediately peaked my interest, by the fact that you can build whatever you want and have potentially millions of people interact with your work. I decided it was time for a change.
   I saved as much money as i could to support me while i learned to code, i quit my job and spent the last 9 months learning code. I promised myself no matter how difficult it got, no matter how bad i wanted to give up to just keep my head down and keep learning.
